CRIMENET / CRIMENET AI

CRIMENET is a Knowledge Graph of nearly 5,000 criminal organizations and the nearly 11,000 edges of cooperation and conflict between them, built from Wikipedia. It is the most extensive catalog of its kind to date.

CRIMENET AI lets you interrogate the whole graph in plain English. It's a GraphRAG system I built from scratch, an advanced AI technique that enhances Large Language Models (LLMs) by integrating Knowledge Graphs.

Like any AI, CRIMENET AI can make mistakes. You can double check any information on the dashboard and the network browser.
Nothing is saved. Copy anything you want to keep before you leave.
