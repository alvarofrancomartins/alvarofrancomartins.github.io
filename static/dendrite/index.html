What is Dendrite?

Dendrite is a context-aware visual branching engine. You give it a starting point — an artist, a dish, a book, anything — and it uses AI to grow a tree of connected recommendations.

What makes it different from a simple "recommend me something" is the path. Each branch remembers how you got there. Starting from Radiohead and drifting toward jazz produces completely different depth-3 results than drifting toward electronic. The path is the context.

The tree is generated in real-time using the DeepSeek LLM. Each node branches into two children, three levels deep — producing 15 connected nodes per exploration. Images are pulled from Wikipedia, iTunes, Open Library, TVMaze, and Jikan depending on the domain.
