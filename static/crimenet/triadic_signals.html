CRIMENET / Triadic Signals

Candidate Undocumented Allies

When two organizations A and B have no direct edge between them but share several of the same cooperation partners or the same adversaries, these common neighbors suggest a real-world relationship that may be missing from Wikipedia. This page lists every such pair. Each common neighbor Cᵢ contributes min(statements A↔Cᵢ, statements B↔Cᵢ) — the minimum number of Wikipedia statements linking each side to that neighbor — and the Total Score is the sum across all shared neighbors, so relationships backed by more evidence weigh proportionally. 2,561 unique pairs: 1,609 with both signals, 486 cooperation-partner-only, 466 adversary-only.
