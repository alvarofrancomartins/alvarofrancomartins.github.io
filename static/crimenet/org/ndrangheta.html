'Ndrangheta

Also known as: Onorata Società, picciotteria
Based in ItalyActive19th century-presentSources'Ndrangheta'Ndrangheta

Italian criminal organization of mafia type originating in Calabria, recognized as a unitary criminal organization with a collegial summit. It is the only mafia present on all five continents, with a global turnover of 150 billion euros according to a 2022 DIA investigation. Active in 42 nations with 400 clans, mainly in Calabria. Its main activity is drug trafficking, followed by public contracts, vote buying, extortion, usury, arms trafficking, gambling, and toxic waste disposal.
